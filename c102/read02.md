# ***Markdown***

## Is a method for arranging the appearance of texts and elements on a website and creating lists of main points and sub-point, you can also make quotes and tables.

### Ex:

### to Bold    * *text * * Or _ _text  _ _
### Italic    * text * or _text  _
### Strikethrough    ~ ~ text ~ ~


# ***GitHub Pages***
